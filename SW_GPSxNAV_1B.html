SW_GPSxNAV_1B

On-board GPSR navigational solution

Thematic areas: Ephemeris

Applicable missions: Swarm

Applicable spacecraft: Swarm-A, Swarm-B, Swarm-C

Description

The GPSxNAV_1B Products contain the spacecraft position and velocity from the on-board navigational solution of the GPSR in the World Geodetic System 1984 (WGS84) reference frame. The product store the Navigational solution in ASCII format, following the Extended Standard Product 3 Orbit Format (SP3c)
