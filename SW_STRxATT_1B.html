SW_STRxATT_1B

Attitude of spacecraft

Thematic areas: Attitude information

Applicable missions: Swarm

Applicable spacecraft: Swarm-A, Swarm-B, Swarm-C

Description

The STRxATT_1B Product contains attitude information at S/C level based on STR data.
