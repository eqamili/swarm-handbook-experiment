SW_MAGx_HR_1B

Magnetic field (50Hz) from VFM

Thematic areas: Magnetic measurements, Space Weather

Applicable missions: Swarm

Applicable spacecraft: Swarm-A, Swarm-B, Swarm-C

Description

The MAGx_HR_1B Product contains magnetic vector data at 50 Hz rate. The time instants of the data are determined by the VFM instrument samplings. No interpolation of the magnetic data is performed, neither to shift the measurements in time nor to fill any gaps in the VFM source data.
