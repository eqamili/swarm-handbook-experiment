SW_EFIx_TCT16

16Hz TII cross-track dataset

Thematic areas: Plasma measurements, Space Weather

Applicable missions: Swarm

Applicable spacecraft: Swarm-A, Swarm-B, Swarm-C

Description

TII Level 1b dataset at 16 Hz cadence containing cross-track ion measurements along with the electric field, magnetic field in the same coordinate frame
