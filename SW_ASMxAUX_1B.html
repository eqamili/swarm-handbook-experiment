SW_ASMxAUX_1B

S/C magnetic stray fields at ASM sensor position

Thematic areas: Magnetic measurements

Applicable missions: Swarm

Applicable spacecraft: Swarm-A, Swarm-B, Swarm-C

Description

The ASMXAUX_1B Product contains detailed information on the S/C magnetic stray fields at the ASM sensor position. The data are provided at 50 Hz at the time-instants of the VFM measurements (for internal practical purposes); the stray field vectors are provided in the ASM sensor frame.
