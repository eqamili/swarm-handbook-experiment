SW_MODx_SC_1B

Satellite Position and velocity from the preliminary Medium Accuracy Orbit Determination

Thematic areas: Ephemeris

Applicable missions: Swarm

Applicable spacecraft: Swarm-A, Swarm-B, Swarm-C

Description

Position and velocity from the preliminary Medium accuracy Orbit Determination (MOD) expressed with respect to the International Terrestrial Reference Frame (ITRF). The product stores the medium precision orbit information in ASCII format, following the Extended Standard Product 3 Orbit Format (SP3c)
