CH_TEC_TMS_2F

Time series of the ionospheric Total Electron Content (TEC) derived for the CHAMP satellite

Thematic areas: Space Weather, Ionosphere/Magnetosphere

Applicable missions: CHAMP

Applicable spacecraft: CHAMP

Description

Integrated electron density along the line of sight from CHAMP to GNSS satellites
